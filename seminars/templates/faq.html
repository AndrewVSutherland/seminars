  2. How do I browse through talks in algebraic topology only?

    At the front page, click "topic" to open the topics menu, press "Clear selection" (if necessary) to deselect all topics, click on "algebraic topology", and click the checkbox to the left of "Restrict by topic". You can also select more than one topic. To close the topics menu, click "topic" again.

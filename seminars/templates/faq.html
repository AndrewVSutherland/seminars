  1. How do I add things to the FAQ?

    Ask questions via the Feedback page!

  2. How do I browse only combinatorics talks?

    At the front page, click "topic" to open the topics menu, press "Clear selection" (if necessary) to deselect all topics, click on "combinatorics", and click the checkbox to the left of "Restrict by topic". You can also select more than one topic. To close the topics menu, click "topic" again.

  3. What should I do if my seminar doesn't meet weekly?

    If it meets every other week on the same day, you can set the Frequency to 14 in the Edit seminar page. If it meets on different days of the week, you won't be able to use the Scheduler page, but you can still add talks one by one.
